PRIVACY POLICY ,TERMS & CONDITIONS

1. Approach to Privacy
As leading information services company, Day ATM, Nadan Payment Services Pvt .Ltd. handles a diverse range of information including our own technical information and information from our customers. We have an established framework for information management that we stringently apply out of respect for the value of the information we are trusted with.
We have established management systems for personal information protection and defined internal rules, a framework for information management, and formulated a privacy policy. They are also consistent with other legislation concerning the handling of personal information and governmental guidelines and standards. We ensure that our directors and employees are continuously informed about these policies, which serve guidelines for our efforts to protect personal information.
2. Day ATM' Privacy Policy
1. Acquisition, Utilization and Provision of Personal Information; Prohibition of Utilization Other than for Intended Purposes Day ATM provides information services, which involve activities through which we keep personal information to our respective business activities, we endeavor to appropriately handle information guided by specific rules about the acquisition, utilization and handling of personal information.
2. Entrusting the Handling of Personal Information
We select a trustee that meets adequate standards for protection. The appropriate measures such as entering into contract(s) with the trustee.
3. Compliance with Legislation and Standards
Day ATM complies with legislation concerning the handling of personal information and governmental guidelines and standards. We ensure that our internal rules for personal information management are consistent with legislation, guidelines and other industry standards.
4. Safety Measures and Corrective Action
Day ATM implements measures to secure the accuracy and safety of personal information, by conforming with rules concerning information security, controlling access to, restricting the means for removal, and preventing unauthorized external access to information.
5. Respect for the Rights of Persons and Their Personal Information If Day ATM Solutions receives a request, complaint or inquiry from a person regarding the disclosure, correction or deletion of their personal information, or regarding the utilization or provision ,we pledge to respond in good faith for the rights of the person to whom the Personal information belongs.
6. Formulation, Implementation, Maintenance and Continual Improvement of Management Systems for Personal Information Protection
Day ATM Solutions will ensure that its directors and employees understand the importance of personal information protection by consistently implementing, maintaining and continuously improving the management systems.
7. About Usage of Cookies and Web beacons A person can refuse to receive a cookie, or can display a warning message when receiving a cookie by changing the setting of a web browser. Moreover, a person can reject a Web beacon by setting to refuse the receipt of a cookie. But, in the first case, please note that a person may not be able to use the all or part of our services provided in the website.
8. Request to Disclose Personal Information or Raising Complaints
When a person wants to request to disclose, correct, add, delete, stop usage, stop provision to a third party, or notify the purpose of usage of his or her personal information held by Day ATM Solutions, or wants to raise complaints, the person is requested to follow the following
procedures:
1. Request to disclose personal information that a person submitted to a Day ATM Solutions' sales department. Please contact the relevant sales department.
2. Request to disclose personal information that a person submitted through our web site. Please contact the department that manages the relevant web site.

2. Please note that Day ATM Solutions is not responsible for the security of personal information in any non- Day ATM Solutions web site whose link is included in the Day ATM Solutions' web site.
